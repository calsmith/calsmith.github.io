I want to report someone
+

Reporting questionable, inappropriate or explicit content on aesthetic is easy. From a user's profile, tap the three dot button (...) and select 'Report' from the popup menu. The user will be investigated by our team.

How is my Instagram used?
+

Your trust is our number one priority.
We use the official Instagram API so we never have access to your email or password and we never post to your account. We display your Instagram posts alongside your aesthetic profile so others can discover your posts. We don't store your Instagram photos and you maintain ownership of all content uploaded to our servers.

You can revoke access at any time in Instagram settings.
