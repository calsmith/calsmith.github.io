Privacy and Terms of Use

Aesthetic is all about meeting great people and starting a conversation. Here are a few things to keep in mind while you use aesthetic.

General Tips

  • The internet can sometimes bring out the negative side of people. If you’re not enjoying yourself, simply leave the chat by unmatching with the other person. Aesthetic cannot be held responsible for the content of messages sent on the service nor can we filter everything uploaded to our servers.
  • Do not use aesthetic if you are a minor. Check with the laws in your country to ensure you are old enough to signup.
  • Not everyone on the internet is who they seem. Please employ common sense and never meet with strangers.
  • Please notify us of abusive or explicit content by tapping the three dots '...' on a profile, then selecting 'Report'.

Information You Provide

To use aesthetic you may provide us with certain information including your name, photos, email address, location and more. Your username does not need to be your real name unless you wish to share your real identity on aesthetic. Sensitive information such as your exact location and email address are never shared with other users or third-parties. Information you provide on your profile including name, gender, and photos are visible to other aesthetic users.

  • Explicit content is strictly forbidden on profiles.
  • We reserve the right to ban users for abusing the service.
  • Don’t disclose personally identifiable information such as your address to other users.

Information Usage

Like most web services, aesthetic stores certain information which is automatically shared with us by your device. This may include details such as your IP address and approximate location. We also store technical information such as details about the device you are using, crash logs and analytical data so that we can improve our services. Information and content you share on the service may be used in other products made by the aesthetic team. Information you provide may be used in an anonymised form to target advertising.

  • Content uploaded to aesthetic will not be shared with third-parties without your consent.
  • Your personal information will never be shared with advertising partners.

Messages and Content

Aesthetic allows you to send messages to other users. Messages and their attached content may be accessible by all members of the chat online and offline. Message content may be stored on our servers in order to sync chat logs and provide a speedy experience. You maintain ownership of any and all content uploaded to our service. Like most other social web services, content you upload may be stored and cached for a reasonable period of time.

Contact Us

We want to provide the best service as humanly possible. If you have any questions, comments, feedback or concerns, please let us know! You can contact us by tapping 'Feedback' in the Settings section.
