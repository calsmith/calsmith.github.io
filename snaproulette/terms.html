Privacy and Terms of Use

Snaproulette is all about meeting people and having great conversations. As you use Snaproulette, here are a few things to keep in mind.

General Tips

  • While anonymity can be fun, it can sometimes bring out a negative side of people. If you’re not enjoying yourself, simply leave the chat and wait to be matched with someone else! Snaproulette cannot be held responsible for the content of messages sent on the service nor can we filter everything uploaded to our servers.
  • Do not use Snaproulette if you are a minor. Check with the laws in your country to ensure you are old enough to signup.
  • Not everyone on the internet is who they seem. Please employ common sense and never meet with strangers.
  • If you would like to report something, please use the ‘Feedback’ button in the app.

Information You Provide

To use Snaproulette you may provide us with certain information including a username and display picture. Your username does not need to be your real name unless you choose to share your real identity on Snaproulette. We don’t require you to provide us with information such as your email, however you may add this information in order to receive additional services. Information you provide on your profile including username, gender, and your display picture are visible to other Snaproulette users.

  • Explicit content in display pictures is strictly forbidden.
  • Don’t disclose personally identifiable information such as your address to other users.

Information Usage

Like most web services, Snaproulette stores certain information which is automatically shared with us by your device. This may include details such as your IP address and approximate location. We also store technical information such as details about the device you are using, crash logs and analytical data so that we can improve our services. Information and content you share on the service may be used in other products made by the Snaproulette team and may be used in advertising.

  • Content uploaded to Snaproulette will not be shared with third-parties without your consent.
  • Your personal information will never be shared with advertising partners.

Messages and Content

Snaproulette allows you to send messages to other users. Messages and their attached content will be accessible by all members of the chat online and offline. Message content may be stored on our servers in order to sync chat logs and provide a speedy experience. You maintain ownership of any and all content uploaded to Snaproulette. Like most other social web services, content you upload may be stored for a reasonable period of time.

Contact Us

We want to provide the best service to our users as humanly possible. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns regarding this page please let us know! You can contact us by swiping right in Snaproulette and tapping 'Feedback'.
